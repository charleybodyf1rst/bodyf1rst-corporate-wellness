## SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
1. **Low Entry Price**: $3/employee/month attracts budget buyers
2. **Challenge Focus**: Well-designed challenge and gamification engine
3. **Easy Setup**: Self-service model requires minimal IT involvement
4. **Bootstrapped Efficiency**: Lean operation, quick decisions
5. **No Minimums (Self-Directed)**: Accessible to smaller companies

### Weaknesses
1. **Basic Mobile App**: Poor user experience limits engagement
2. **No Coaching**: Zero AI or live coaching capabilities
3. **Limited Scope**: Challenges-focused, not holistic wellness
4. **Add-on Pricing**: Core features require extra fees
5. **Full-Service Threshold**: 500+ employees for premium support
6. **No Mental Health**: Missing key wellness dimension

### Opportunities
- Mobile app improvement
- AI integration potential
- Coaching feature addition
- SMB market expansion

### Threats
- Competitors with comprehensive features at similar price
- Feature commoditization in challenge space
- Mobile experience expectations rising
- Holistic wellness trend leaving them behind
