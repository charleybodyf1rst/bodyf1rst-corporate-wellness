## Common Objections & Responses

**"Our employees love Headspace"**
> "Great content, but employees also need fitness and nutrition support. You're paying premium for one dimension of wellness. For comparable cost, we deliver complete wellness including mental health, with AI coaching that adapts to each person."

**"Mental health is our priority"**
> "Ours too - which is why we include it. But research shows mental health improves 40% more when combined with physical exercise. Why choose one when you can have both for the same investment?"

**"We want the brand recognition"**
> "Results matter more than brand. Our 85% engagement rate proves employees value the platform. And when you need fitness, nutrition, or coaching, Headspace can't help - you'd need another vendor."
